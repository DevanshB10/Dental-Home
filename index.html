Our services

We provide to you the best choices for you. Adjust it to your health needs and make sure your undergo treatment with a highly qualified doctor who you can trust
  • Veneers and Smile Designing
    Smile designing involves a comprehensive approach to enhancing the aesthetics of a person's smile by addressing factors like tooth shape, size, color, and alignment through procedures like veneers, dental bonding, and contouring.
  • Dental Implants
    Dental implants are titanium posts surgically placed into the jawbone to replace missing teeth roots. These implants provide a stable foundation for attaching replacement teeth, such as crowns, bridges, or dentures.
  • Crown and Bridge Work
    Crown and bridge work involves the use of dental crowns (caps) and bridges to restore damaged or missing teeth. Crowns cover a single damaged tooth to strengthen and improve its appearance, while bridges consist of one or more artificial teeth supported by adjacent healthy teeth or dental implants, filling gaps left by missing teeth.
  • Painless Extractions
    Painless extractions refer to the removal of teeth with minimal discomfort or pain for the patient. We use modern techniques, local anesthesia, and sometimes sedation to ensure a virtually painless experience during tooth extraction procedures.
  • Braces/Orthodontic Treatment
    Braces and orthodontic treatment are designed to correct misaligned teeth and jaws. Braces consist of brackets and wires that apply gentle pressure to gradually shift teeth into proper alignment. Orthodontic treatment enhances both aesthetics and oral function, improving the bite and overall dental health.
  • Ultrasonic Scaling
    Ultrasonic scaling is an advanced dental cleaning technique that uses high-frequency vibrations to remove plaque, tartar, and stains from the surfaces of teeth and below the gumline. The ultrasonic scaler's gentle vibrations break down deposits, making the cleaning process more efficient and comfortable for patients, while also promoting gum health.
